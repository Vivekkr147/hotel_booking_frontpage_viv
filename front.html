Welcome to Grand Horizon

A Legacy of Luxury Since 1995

A Symbol of Unique architecture, Grand Horizon Hotel stands tall behind Karmapa Mandir in Bodhgaya "The World Heritage" site. The hotel caters to the diversified needs of tourists from France, Thailand, Taiwan, Sri Lanka, Korea, China, Japan and other International tourists as well as domestic leisure travelers. Bodhgaya is unique for its Buddhist pilgrimage tours. The Hotel has 27 A/C rooms with all the modern amenities. "Grand Horizon Hotel" Restaurant serves Indian, Chinese, Continental, Tandoori, Thai food and much more...! In order to provide comfort to our esteemed guests, the entire hotel has been specially designed to cater various needs. Furniture, Restaurant, Room Service, Bathrooms and all other amenities are desinged to meet the customer needs.
